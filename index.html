Meet Stephen.

Stephen Lord Building & Design is a home construction company with a long tradition of quality carpentry and fine craftsmanship. Since 1990, we have taken great pride in our satisfied customers. We provide everything from whole house renovations to small home improvement projects, tailoring each job to suit your needs, and offering you comprehensive advice along the way.
