Frequently Asked Questions

Some common questions our customers ask.

How should I structure a question?

Try to keep questions to one line only, short and sweet. Use sentence case only.

How should I structure an answer?

If possible, begin with a one or two word answer. Then follow up with more details. Try not to exceed three lines.

How many questions should I offer?

The minimum amount you need in order to get the job done sufficiently. Try not to exceed ten.

Do I need an even amount of questions in each cell?

Nope. As long as both cells end at exactly the same height, you should be fine.

How long ago did dinosaurs live?

The time difference between when T-Rrex lived is greater than the time difference between Tyrannosaurus and now.

Am I rich?

Over 3 billion people live on less than $2.50 a day, so yes, you probably are rich.
